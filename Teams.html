INTERIOR DESIGN

RH Interior Design is a fully integrated, concierge-level interior design platform that inspires clients to imagine the infinite potential of a blank canvas and make their dream space a reality.
The evolution of our interior design services positions RH as a leading interior design firm and demonstrates how our brand can deliver value that transcends just creating and selling product, as we move towards conceptualizing and selling spaces.
RH Interior Designers place clients at the center of the creative process, working to realize the full potential of their home and bring their vision to life. Trained in the RH design ethos and educated in architecture, interior design and the arts, the RH Design Team helps clients reimagine and reinvent one room or an entire home, inside and out.
From striking landscape design to fine Italian bed linens, RH’s entire product assortment is leveraged – allowing for the creation of harmonious spaces that reflect an intentional approach to designing the whole space, rather than decorating with a mix of individual pieces.

DELIVERY & DISTRIBUTION CENTERS

We receive daily shipments of RH product from around the globe. As a Distribution Center associate, you will chart the course for our products by ensuring they are carefully received, packed and prepared for delivery.
In partnership with our Distribution Centers, RH Home Delivery Logistics teams complete the final step in our customer experience. By providing a white-glove service to our customers, our associates are empowered to ensure our customers are delighted with their experience.
Distribution Center and Home Delivery Logistics associates have access to a flexible work schedule, as well as an onsite gym in some locations.
